Anxiety is most certainly a normal emotion. Many have felt the nervocity facing 
issues at work, prior to that exam or when taking a seemingly important decision. 
Anxiety helps us to direct these choices and to advance our lives...
Imagine that the aforementioned state of stress is permanent. Life still advances,
but you're left behind with a continuous struggle that sucks all the energy out 
of your being. Your light is silently fading, yet the challenges keep on coming.

This game was designed by me to try and explain what my anxiety disorder entails.
